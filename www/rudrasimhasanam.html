RUDRA SIMHASANAM

'Rudra Simhasanam' is a tantric thriller directed by Shibu Gangadharan and scripted by Sunil Parameswaran. The movie is an adaptation of Sunil Parameswaran's novel "Raudrathalam". Suresh Gopi is seen in a new avatar as Rudra Simhan, a yogi from the Himalayas.
